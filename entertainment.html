Come on down to the river for a delicious dinner at Jumpin' Jack's and prepare to be impressed by the amazing talent of the US Water Ski Show Team. The water ski shows will begin when the weather gets warmer and take place every Tuesday around 6 pm! The shows last about an hour. Don't forget to bring chairs or a blanket and arrive early for a good seat- the shows are very popular!


Fireworks

Freedom Park

Freedom Park fills up on summer nights offering free live music through its Summer Concert Series, which highlights performers from the Capital Region and beyond. The park is tobacco and alcohol free and it is recommended that concert attendees bring blankets and chairs.
